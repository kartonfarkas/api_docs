E-Mail Status Codes
===================

The Email Status ID code indicates the current status of an email and can be one of the following:

.. list-table:: E-Mail Status Codes
   :header-rows: 1
   :widths: 10 40

   * - Status
     - Description
   * - 1
     - In design
   * - 2
     - Tested
   * - 3
     - Launched
   * - 4
     - Ready to launch
   * - -3
     - Deactivated
